WHAT IS HOTSPOT BUSINESS

hotspot business involves providing public or private Wi-Fi access to users, typically through a wireless local area network (WLAN) using a router connected to an internet service provider. Businesses, communities, or individuals can set up these hotspots, often in areas with high foot traffic like cafes, hotels, or public spaces, to offer internet access to customers or residents.

HOW TO START HOTSPOT BUSINESS

To start a hotspot business, you'll need to acquire internet service, purchase or lease networking equipment (router, access points, etc.), and establish a system for managing user access and payments.
